Calculation of the monthly installment and the total amount for the repayment of a loan. 
Based on the entered loan amount (principal), the annual interest rate and the repayment period expressed in months.

Project objectives: 
To allow the user to enter data: Loan amount (denar), Annual interest rate (%), Repayment period (in months) 
To calculate: Monthly installment (EMI) Total repayment amount 
To display the results in the output in a clear and readable manner
